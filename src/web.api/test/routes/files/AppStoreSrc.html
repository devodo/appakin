Description

Top-ranked Yelp for your iPhone or iPad has over 50 million reviews on businesses worldwide — all in the palm of your hand. Whether you are looking for a pizzeria that is open now or a coffee shop nearby, Yelp is your local guide to finding just the place to eat, shop, drink, relax, and play.

• Discover great local businesses.
• Search for nearby restaurants, shops, and services.
• Filter search results by neighborhood, distance, rating, price, and what’s open now.
• Read millions of reviews written by a community of active, expert locals.
• Get to know a business through beautiful photos.
• Find great Deals offered by your favorite local businesses.
• Look up addresses and phone numbers, call a business, or make reservations directly from the app.
• Write reviews, check-in to businesses, upload photos, and add tips of your own!

Birthday Disaster
     
by True Foodie

It was my husband's birthday, and we were hoping for an enjoyable evening. However, everything went down hill as soon as we were seated. We ordered wine which was spilled all over the table. The waiter did apologize. The cloth was not replaced. The appetizer dishes remained on the table until we asked for them to be removed. Wine & water glasses we not refilled unless we asked or did it ourselves. Totally unacceptable. The food was good but the quality did not equal to the price. Dessert was grocery store quality and delivered without utensils. The waiter finally showed up with forks. Not worth the wait. The bill was the only thing that showed up in a timely fashion. This place is like dining in a fancy cafeteria. I would not recommend or ever return.
